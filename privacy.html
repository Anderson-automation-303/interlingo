Privacy Policy

Interlingo is a Discord translation bot designed to help users communicate across different languages.

1. Information We Process

Interlingo may process the following information:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server (Guild) IDs
  • Message content submitted for translation
  • Error logs and technical data used for troubleshooting

2. Translation Processing

To provide translations, message content may be transmitted to third-party translation services such as DeepL. These services process the content solely to return translated results.

Interlingo does not permanently store original messages or translated content after processing is completed.

3. Data Retention

Interlingo does not intentionally retain message content beyond what is required to provide translations, enforce rate limits, or troubleshoot technical issues.

4. Third-Party Services

Interlingo relies on third-party services, including DeepL and hosting providers, to operate the bot. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Future Premium Features

Interlingo may introduce optional premium features in the future, such as saved language preferences. If such features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect what data is stored and how it is used.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data, but no system is completely secure. Use of Interlingo is at your own discretion.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Interlingo after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit the Support page or contact the Interlingo development team.
